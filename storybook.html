Enhance Consistency in Learning Flow and User Engagement in Online Code Learning

Research Tablet 2023 12 weeks

Overview

In light of integrated multimodal generative AI, this project explores the potential of using AI as an assistant for parents and educators in guiding children through reading storybooks, especially in language-learning contexts.


Leveraging computer vision and NLP, the co-pilot analyzes and synthesizes relationships between text and illustrations in storybooks, enabling parents and educators to enhance children’s linguistic, cognitive, and cultural development.


Unlike existing EdTech tools, which primarily focus on engagement or learning outcomes, this co-pilot emphasizes the deeper educational value of storybooks, equipping adults with insights to create rich, meaningful reading experiences for children.
