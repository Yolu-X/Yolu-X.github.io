Enhance Consistency in Learning Flow and User Engagement in Online Code Learning

Client Project Website 2024 10 weeks

Overview

HackQuest is an online learning platform specializing in Web3 development. In collaboration with various Web3 ecosystems, it offers diverse learning tracks, hands-on projects, and additional resources like hackathons, a learning community, and job-hunting support for aspiring Web3 developers.


Following the 2022 launch, HackQuest is now focused on iteration based on initial user feedback. Our team is prioritizing improvements in the core learning experience to address issues of inconsistency, low engagement, and limited discoverability of learning materials, along with the introduction of a note-taking feature to support user learning.
